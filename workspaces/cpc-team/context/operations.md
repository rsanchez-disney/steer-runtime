# CPC Operations

## Scope

- Capacity planning and utilization tracking
- POD composition and allocation visibility
- Cross-vertical dependency management
- Team metadata extraction and reporting
- Governance compliance checks (Jira hygiene, review SLAs)

## Capacity Model

- Utilization target: 85% (15% buffer for unplanned work)
- Capacity reviewed monthly by PM + TM

## Cross-Vertical Coordination

- CPC interfaces with partner verticals through Delivery Manager and Delivery Director
- Dependency conflicts escalated within 24 hours
- Shared component changes require notification to all consuming domains

## Data Sources

- team_context.md (roster and domain structure)
- governance.md (operational expectations, SLAs)
- Jira (ticket hygiene, sprint metrics)
- workspace.json (managed studios, jira prefixes)
