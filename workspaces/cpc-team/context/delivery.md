# CPC Delivery

## Scope

- Sprint burndown and velocity reporting
- Intake request processing and prioritization
- Stakeholder status updates and summaries
- Cross-domain dependency tracking
- Release readiness assessments
- Capacity allocation visibility

## Workflows

### Sprint Reporting
- Jira data from IEXP, AEXP, COREEXP projects
- Velocity tracked per POD
- Burndown reviewed at mid-sprint and sprint close

### Intake Process
- New requests assessed for capacity and domain fit
- PM + TM alignment before commitment
- Stakeholder notified within 48 hours of intake

### Stakeholder Communication
- Weekly status to Disney Managers per domain
- Escalation path: PM → Delivery Manager → Delivery Director
- Monthly executive summary to VP

## Data Sources

- Jira (IEXP, AEXP, COREEXP projects)
- team_context.md (roster and workstreams)
- governance.md (review processes and SLAs)
