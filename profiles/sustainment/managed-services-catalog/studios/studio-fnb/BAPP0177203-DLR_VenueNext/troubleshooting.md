# Troubleshooting — DLR VenueNext

## Common Issues

### Issue: Orders not reaching kitchen displays

**Symptoms:** Orders submitted but DLR kitchen doesn't see them

**Root Cause:** VenueNext internal issue or Pusher disconnection

**Resolution:**
1. Check Pusher: https://status.pusher.com/
2. Email help@venuenext.com with: site=DLR, time range, order UUIDs, impact
3. Teams: External - SkyTab Venue
