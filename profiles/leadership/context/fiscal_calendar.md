# Fiscal Calendar

Disney follows the U.S. Federal Government fiscal year (October – September).

## Quarters

| Quarter | Start | End |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Q1 | October 1 | December 31 |
| Q2 | January 1 | March 31 |
| Q3 | April 1 | June 30 |
| Q4 | July 1 | September 30 |

## FY Naming

The fiscal year is labeled by the calendar year in which it ends:
- FY2026 = October 1, 2025 – September 30, 2026
- Q2 FY2026 = January 1 – March 31, 2026

## How to Determine Current Quarter

Given today's date:
- Jan–Mar → Q2 of FY ending this calendar year
- Apr–Jun → Q3 of FY ending this calendar year
- Jul–Sep → Q4 of FY ending this calendar year
- Oct–Dec → Q1 of FY ending next calendar year
