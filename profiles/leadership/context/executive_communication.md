# Executive Communication Guidelines

## Audience Adaptation

### Disney Director / VP
- Lead with business impact, not technical details
- Use metrics that map to business outcomes (revenue impact, customer experience, time-to-market)
- Keep to 1 page / 5 minutes reading time
- End with clear ask or decision needed
- Tone: confident, concise, forward-looking

### Colleagues (Peer Directors / Managers)
- Include technical context and architecture decisions
- Share learnings and patterns that could help their teams
- Be transparent about challenges and what you're trying
- Tone: collaborative, open, practical

### Partners (External teams, vendors)
- Focus on integration points, API changes, timeline commitments
- Be specific about what you need from them and by when
- Include technical specs where relevant
- Tone: professional, precise, commitment-oriented

## Structure Rules
- Always start with the conclusion/recommendation
- Support with 2-3 data points
- End with next steps and owners
- Never exceed the audience's attention span
