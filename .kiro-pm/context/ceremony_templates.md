# Ceremony Templates

## Sprint Planning
1. Review sprint goal
2. Pull stories from prioritized backlog
3. Confirm estimates and acceptance criteria
4. Assign owners
5. Confirm team capacity
6. Commit to sprint scope

## Daily Standup
Per team member:
1. What did you complete yesterday?
2. What are you working on today?
3. Any blockers?

Team level:
- Sprint burndown check
- Blocker resolution actions

## Sprint Review
1. Sprint goal recap
2. Demo completed stories
3. Stakeholder feedback
4. Backlog adjustments

## Retrospective
1. Review sprint metrics
2. Check previous action items
3. What went well?
4. What didn't go well?
5. Action items (max 3, with owners and deadlines)
